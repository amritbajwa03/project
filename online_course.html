Welcome to Online Safety Course

Learn to how to stay safe online, protect your personal information, and avoid common online threats.

Recognising Online Scams

Common Types of Scams

  • Online Shopping Scams: Fake websites or sellers offering items that do not exists.
  • Tech Support Scams: Attackers pretending to be from well known companies, claiming your device is infected.
  • Investment Scams: Promises of high returns with no risk.

How to Stay Safe

  • Shop on trusted websites
  • Look for spelling mistakes or unusual language.
  • Be sceptical of unsolicited phone calls offering tech support.
  • Never transfer money to people you don't know.

Protecting Your Data and Devices

  • Install antivirus software.
  • Enable automatic updates for your operating system and apps.
  • Use a secure home Wi-Fi network which has a strong password.
  • Regular back up important files to an external hard drive or cloud service.
  • Test back ups to ensure you can restore your data if needed.

Safe Online Browsing

Identifying Secure Websites

  • Look for 'https://' and a padlocl symbol in the address bar of the browser.
  • Be cautious of websites with strange domain names and spelling errors.

Browser Settings

  • Enable enhanced security in your browser settings.
  • Block pop-ups and enable anti-phishing filters.
