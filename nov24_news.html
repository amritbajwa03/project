November 2024

In November 2024, several new cyber threats targeted individuals in the UK. Staying aware of these scams is crucial to protect yourself and your information.

Parcel Delivery Scams

Scammers are sending fake delivery texts pretending to be from Royal Mail or courier companies. The texts claim you need to pay a small fee to release your parcel. Avoid clicking any links, and report suspicious messages
