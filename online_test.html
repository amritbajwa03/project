Online Safety Quiz

1. Which of the following is a sign of an online shopping scam?

  • A) A website offering extremely low prices for high-value items
  • B) A website with customer reviews and a secure payment option
  • C) A website that has been operating for several years

2. What is a tech support scam?

  • A) A legitimate service offered by your device manufacturer
  • B) A scam where attackers pretend to be from well-known companies claiming your device is infected
  • C) A government service that provides cybersecurity advice

3. Which of the following is a common characteristic of investment scams?

  • A) A guarantee of high returns with no risk
  • B) A requirement to sign up for an official financial institution
  • C) A legitimate investment opportunity backed by regulated financial bodies

4. Which is the best way to protect yourself from online scams?

  • A) Trust every email or phone call that claims to be from a well-known company
  • B) Be cautious of unsolicited messages and verify information from official sources
  • C) Immediately send personal information to verify your identity

5. What is the best way to keep your devices secure?

  • A) Install antivirus software and enable automatic updates
  • B) Only use free public Wi-Fi networks
  • C) Ignore software updates because they slow down your device

6. What should you do to protect your Wi-Fi network at home?

  • A) Use a strong password and enable encryption
  • B) Keep the default router password and allow public access
  • C) Turn off password protection to make it easier for guests to connect

7. What does https:// in a website’s URL indicate?

  • A) The website is 100% safe and trustworthy
  • B) The website is encrypted, making data transfer more secure
  • C) The website is fake and should be avoided

8. Which of these is NOT a safe online browsing habit?

  • A) Looking for spelling mistakes and strange domain names in URLs
  • B) Clicking on pop-up ads that offer free prizes
  • C) Blocking pop-ups and enabling anti-phishing filters

9. Why is it important to regularly back up your important files?

  • A) It ensures you have a copy in case of cyberattacks or device failure
  • B) It makes your computer faster
  • C) It increases your internet speed

10. If you receive an email claiming to be from Amazon asking you to verify your account by clicking a link, what should you do?

  • A) Click the link to verify your account immediately
  • B) Ignore the email and delete it
  • C) Go directly to Amazon’s official website and check your account
